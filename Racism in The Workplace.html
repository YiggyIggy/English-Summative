The Story of Indigenous Peoples

Many indigenous peoples are forced to share similar stories. with 40% of indigenous children living in poverty and indigenous women being twice as likely to be in poverty compared to non-indigenous women. with poverty in indigenous communities being more prevelant in the northern part of canada. With extreme temperatures and a lack of infrastructure many indigenous peoples die due to lack of quality medical care
