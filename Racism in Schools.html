Lewis's school life

Lewis in in an advanced course at his highschool. As the only kid from the reservation in his advanced course he procures very few friends. Having a self described "invisible bubble" keeping them at bay. The only friend he has has grandparents that worked in "Indian Boarding Schools" and only talked to him to relate with his dad.
