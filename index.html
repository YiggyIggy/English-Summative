About the book

"If I ever get out of here" is a book about a highschool honors student named "Lewis Blake" who lives in a crumbling home on a reservation but goes to a mostly white highschool. The book explores Lewis's journey through highschool as he has to endure challenges such as racism, poverty and the fear of his non-indigenous new best friend George haddonfield finding out his poverty.

About the Author

The author Eric Gansworth is an american of Tuscarora descent and is a citizen of the Onondaga Nation. He has written five books with two of them winning awards called: "Mending skins" and "Extra Indians"
